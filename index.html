Our Architecture


        

          A look inside our intelligent system that processes geospatial data to deliver actionable insights.
        


        

          

            
          


          

            

              
1. Occurrence Prediction (O_PRED)

              
When a user selects coordinates, our model analyzes historical fire data, weather patterns, and vegetation density to calculate the initial probability at that location.

            


            

              
2. Threat Prediction (T_PRED)

              
Simultaneously, a second model assesses potential damage using proximity to infrastructure, water bodies, and forest coverage to determine overall risk level.

            


            

              
3. Output & Visualization

              
Both outputs are synthesized into a comprehensive risk & damage analysis and visualized on the map for fast, informed decisions.

Our Business Model

        

          Our strategy is centered on a multi-faceted approach to create a sustainable and impactful business. We aim to collaborate with partners while building diverse revenue streams to fuel our growth and innovation.
        


        

          

            
Subscription Models

            
Offering tiered subscription plans for governments, NGOs, and large enterprises, providing access to advanced analytics, priority support, and higher data resolution.

Our Future Roadmap

        

          Innovation is at our core. Our roadmap outlines a phased approach to creating a fully autonomous wildfire detection and response system.
        


        

          
          

            
Phase 1 (Current)

            
Predictive Intelligence

            
Leveraging historical and real-time data to accurately predict wildfire occurrence and spread, providing the foundational insights for proactive measures.

          


          
          

            

              
            

          


          
          

            
Phase 2 (Future)

            
Autonomous Verification & Response

            
A groundbreaking step towards automation:

            
    
              
  • Drone Deployment: Upon high-probability prediction, automated drones are dispatched.
    • 
              
  • Visual Confirmation: Drones stream live imagery to our cloud platform.
    • 
              
  • AI Analysis: A CNN/YOLO model analyzes the feed to confirm fire presence and severity.
    • 
              
  • Automated Response: The system notifies authorities with precise data, recommending an appropriate response.
